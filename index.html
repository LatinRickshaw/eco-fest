Take Part as a Stallholder

We'd love community groups, charities, and local organisations to join us. Here's everything you need to know:

  • Entry is free for the public and stallholders alike.
  • Arrive from 10am to set up; please clear away by 4pm.
  • Pitches are allocated on arrival — both indoor and outdoor space is available.
  • There is no mains electrical power on site. Generators are permitted if silenced and secured within a safety barrier.
  • Please bring your own stand, display, gazebo, tables, and chairs, and remove all your own refuse at the end of the day.
  • Public Liability Insurance is provided by {{ site.organiser }} (this excludes unaccompanied minors and dangerous equipment).
  • There's a Speaker's Corner with 15-minute slots for talks on environmental topics — request a slot when you register.
